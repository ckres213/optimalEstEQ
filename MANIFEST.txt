Clean, updated source bundle for the bivariate Hawkes Godambe simulation.

Important updates in this bundle:
- The final manuscript figure is generated by make_article_bivar_pair.R.
- The final side-by-side figure uses a dedicated bottom legend strip.
- submit_slurm_combine_figures_bivar.sh generates paper_bivar_efficiency_pair_Tge1000.pdf/png.
- run_quick_pilot_bivar.sh also tests the side-by-side figure generator.
- Slurm fallback array counts match the #SBATCH arrays: population 64, estimators 400.

The generated outputs/logs/results directories are intentionally not included.
